He was also a Core Team member, in the Operations division at the Counselling Service. Owing to his experiences he has chosen to pursue higher studies(PhD) in the field of Bio-Engineering.

Nanyang Technological University is the second oldest public autonomous research university of Singapore. One of the best in the world, this university was ranked 12th in the World and 3rd in Asia by QS World university ranking and is the 3rd best university in the world for Material Sciences in particular.
